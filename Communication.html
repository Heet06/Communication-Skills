What is communication?

Communication is the activity of coveying information through the exchange of thoughts, messages or information by speech, visuals, signals, writing or behavior. It is the meaningful exchange of information between two groups or a group of persons.

How is a process of Communication successful?

Communication is considered successful when the real idea is interpreted from the sender to receiver using appropriate channel and meaning of the message is clearly understood to the receiver otherwise it is considered as miscommunication.
