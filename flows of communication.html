Flows of communication

When communication takes place, the message flows from one person to other. Communication can be classified as per its flow in the following manner:

1.Downward communication:

ommunicatin from a person with senior authority to a person with junior autority such as instructions, tasks orders,etc.

Example: When a manager askes an employee to restock the shelves.

2.Upward Communication

This type of communication can be categorised as the opposite of downward communication such as requests,proposals, feedback,etc.

Example: An employee drafting a proposal for a raise.

3.Horizontal communication

This types of communication takes place between members of group when all of them are peers or at the same lavel.

Example: The managers of diffrent departments talking about their employees.

4.Crosswise communication

This type of communication can flow in any and all directions.

Example:When a student asks the teacher a quastion and vise versa.
