General and Technical Communication

General Communication:

In everyday life when we communicate with one another, we use the language of general communication whereas when speaking to a professional person about technical matters, we have to ues technical language.

Technical Communication:

During technical communication, one has to use a set pattern and follow a formal approach. General communication does not follow these patterns and is often informal in its nature. Technical communication is meant for a specific set of people who can follow and comprehend the technical language being coveyed.

Example:

If a person goes to a supermarket and ask for'solanum lycopersicum', he/she will get perplexed looks from the vendor whereas if we ask for it with its more common name 'tomato', the response from the vendor will be quick and normal.
